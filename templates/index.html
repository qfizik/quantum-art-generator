This app generates art from a textual input by harnessing noise on a quantum computer! 'Noise' in this context refers to how the output of a real quantum computer tends to stray from what you would ideally expect due to how sensitive its components are.

What you see below is the bubble representation of "Live long and prosper" plus a little bit of quantum noise!

There are two ways you can create your very own quantum bubble art:

  • Preset Noise: This generates a slideshow of artworks with preset levels of noise. You can observe what happens as noise levels increase!
  • Custom Noise: This generates a single piece of artwork from your own specified noise levels.

As noise levels increase, more bubbles and more colors are introduced. You don't need to understand quantum noise in order to make the art, but if you'd like to understand what's happening, please check out the article: How I Use Quantum Computing to Create Art.
